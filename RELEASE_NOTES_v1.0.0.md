## Scientific scope

This release formalizes transient rational locking in modular logarithmic orbits as an arithmetic-dynamics problem. Apparent geometric skeletons are treated as transient shadows of rational approximants to logarithmic rotation numbers, with persistence governed by a torus-sawtooth dephasing law.

2. Modulus as Lens, Not Composer
3. Inversion Symmetry of Spectral Fingerprints
